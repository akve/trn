Link Amazon Profile

In order to use Trust Review Network, you must link your Amazon profile to your Trust Review Network profile. This does not give us access to your Amazon account, it only allows us to verify that you are a legitimate Amazon shopper.

Where do I find my Amazon ID?

Go to Amazon.com if prompted, login. Once you have arrived on your Profile, copy the url and paste it in the input on the left. Your amazon look like this : https://www.amazon.com/gp/profile/{ AMAZONID }.
